How redemption works

  • Open the app and tap Redeem Offer.
  • Scan the brewery’s QR code with the built-in scanner.
  • Show the redemption screen to staff.
  • Tap Yes, Confirm when prompted.
  • The reward is stamped and cannot be redeemed again for that brewery location that year.

What kinds of rewards are offered?

Offers vary by brewery and state law. Depending on the location, rewards may include BOGO beers, discounted beer, discounted food, merchandise perks, or another special taproom offer.
