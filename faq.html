FAQ

Questions from app users and brewery partners.

This page brings together the most important explanations from your current pages so visitors can find answers quickly.

For users

App questions

How do I redeem an offer?

Open the app, tap Redeem Offer, scan the brewery’s QR code, and show the confirmation screen to staff.

Can I redeem the same brewery offer more than once?

No. The offer is designed to be one-time per user for that brewery location and is stamped with a date and timestamp after confirmation.

Are all rewards beer discounts?

No. Rewards vary by brewery and state law. Some locations may offer discounts on food, merchandise, or another taproom perk instead.

Do I need to be at the brewery to use the QR code?

Yes. The QR flow is meant for in-person redemption during checkout or while staff can verify the confirmation screen.

For breweries

Partner questions

Do we need any special equipment?

No. The program is designed to work without POS integration or special hardware.

Can we choose our own offer?

Yes. Breweries choose their own offer, which can be adjusted to fit state rules and their preferred promotion style.

Should each location submit separately?

Yes. The current setup asks for a separate signup for each brewery location so each listing can be handled correctly.

Can brewery partners download the app?

Yes. Partners can download the app to view their listing. The printed QR code is for customer redemption inside the app, not a standalone coupon.
