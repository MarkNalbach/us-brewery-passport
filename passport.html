US Brew Passport — For Beer Lovers

Discover breweries. Track your visits. Unlock one-time rewards.

The 2026 US Brew Passport is a nationwide craft beer passport designed for people who love exploring breweries. One app. One passport. Breweries across the U.S.

Using the Passport is Simple

  • Download the US Brew Passport app
  • Browse participating breweries by state or map
  • Visit a brewery in person
  • Scan the brewery’s QR code with staff present
  • Show the confirmation screen to redeem your reward
  • The visit is stamped with date & time — one redemption per year

Each brewery sets its own reward — typically a BOGO, percentage off, or a fun house perk.

Premium Access Unlocks:

  • Access to all participating breweries nationwide
  • One-time rewards at each brewery per year
  • Visit tracking with digital stamps
  • Notes for your favorite beers & breweries
  • No paper passport to lose

Whether you’re traveling, visiting family, or exploring your hometown, the US Brew Passport helps you find your next great beer.
