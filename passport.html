New breweries are being added daily.

Can I redeem more than once?

No. Each brewery can be redeemed once per user per year.

Do I have to be at the brewery?

Yes. Redemption requires scanning the brewery’s QR code with staff present.

Can I see breweries before purchasing?

Yes. You can browse participating breweries before upgrading.
