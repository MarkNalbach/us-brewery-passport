Redemption demo

See the staff-verified redemption flow.

Redemption is designed to be quick, clear for staff, and locked after confirmation so the same reward cannot be reused.

How redemption works

  • Guests open the app and tap Redeem Offer.
  • The built-in scanner opens and they scan your brewery’s QR code.
  • Your staff sees the redemption screen instantly.
  • The confirmation screen clearly displays your brewery’s name.
  • After tapping Yes, Confirm, the reward is stamped, locked, and cannot be redeemed again.

It’s fast, simple, and designed to protect your brewery while creating a seamless experience for guests.
