Brewery partner program

Bring more visitors to your taproom with a simple QR-based reward system.

US Brew Passport helps breweries get featured in a nationwide brewery discovery app while keeping redemption easy for staff. Guests redeem in person, the offer is confirmed on screen, and each reward is designed to be one-time per user for that location.

Complete the partner form See the user-facing page
Brewery partners are welcome to download the app to view their listing. The table-tent QR code is for in-taproom redemption and does not do anything by itself unless the guest taps Redeem Offer inside the app.
Why breweries join

A low-friction way to get discovered

  • Free exposure in a nationwide craft beer discovery app
  • More foot traffic from users specifically looking for brewery stops
  • No POS integration or special equipment needed
  • Flexible offers based on your location and local rules
  • Staff-verified redemption designed to prevent reuse
How it works for breweries

Simple on the guest side. Simple on the staff side.

This section pulls together the clearest points from your current brewery page and turns them into a more traditional site layout.

A user visits your brewery

They arrive with the app and open your listing inside US Brew Passport.

They tap Redeem Offer

The app opens the built-in scanner and the user scans your QR code in the taproom.

Staff verifies the screen

The confirmation screen clearly displays your brewery name so staff can verify the redemption.

The reward is locked

Once confirmed, the visit is marked redeemed with a timestamp and cannot be used again by that same user.

Offer flexibility

You choose the reward that fits your taproom.

Most breweries offer a BOGO or discounted beer where allowed. In states with restrictions, food, merchandise, or another taproom perk can be used instead.

Common examples

  • Buy one beer, get one free
  • 50% off two beers
  • 50% off the second beer
  • Discount on food or another approved perk

What to submit

  • Brewery name and address
  • Main contact information
  • Your chosen offer details
  • Separate signup for each location
